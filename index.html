About Me

My favorite type of food is tex-mex, while my favorite dessert is Hummingbird cake, and my favorite breakfast item is grits. Another thing to note is that one of my favorite hobbies is studying history, since I enjoy looking at stories of the past, with a particular liking of the Mongol Conquests and the Early Byzantine Empire.

My Future Career Plans

My current major is a BS in Geography, as I do really enjoy both nature and believe in conservation efforts, which are best kept up not by activisim, but by having people in positions that can actually use tht power to help the wilderness. Another reason I chose Geography as my major is because even if I don't love what I am doing, I should be able to keep doing it as long as it is something important that matters. Finally, the reason I chose a BS rather than a BA in geography is that the BS just gives you more options after you graduate versus just getting a BA.

My current minor is Education as I believe that education is very important to the health of society and believe that doing so would be a good thing overall. I also chose education as I have a passion for and very much like to learn new and different things, so being able to impart that on a new generation would be a good thing. Finally, I chose it as my minor since if for some reason I can't find work with a geography degree, I will never be out of work If I can work as a teacher.
